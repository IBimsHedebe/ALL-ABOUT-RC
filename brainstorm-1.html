Sketch for a Stub axle steering system

First Sketch of a simple steering mechanism that i came up with, afterward I searched for a similar design and adopted the name.

The design works by shifting LA via the servo to the left or right, wich in case rotates the tires on the axle in RA, where it is mounted to.

The Servo is a fixed component, it is mounted directly to the chassis. Where as LA and the tires are partly mounted and still able to move in the desired direction.

The red Dots showcase wich part is connected to each other and cannot move independently from each other. The Arrows on the other hand indicate the direction of movement.
